Digital Publishing

Publishing is significant to me because I am interested in publishing a book one day. Knowing the pros and cons between traditional and digital publishing is essential in making the right decision to benefit any creative– and to understand the current climate of publishing, it is important to understand how we got here. .

Computers have not only changed how authors publish their works, but also how they produce them. Digital printers have made printing books on demand possible, so consumers can access the books they want efficiently, and publishers and authors don’t waste their money printing too many books than necessary.

Even if an author only wanted to offer their books in a physical format, there are so many digital steps in the production process in order to turn a rough manuscript into an edited version that is in the right format for the kind of physical book– whether that be a hardcover or paper back. In the digital age all books go through an extensive technological process in order for them to be ready to be printed and then finally sold.
